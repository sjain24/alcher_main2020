Clay modelling Competition

GC Bounties: 100

"Love to play with clay? This is your chance to make models with the help of clay."

  • Rules

1. Participants need to make clay models based on the theme given by the organiser.

2. Organisers will be providing clays of 4 different colours.

3. Time limit is 3 hours.

4. It is a solo event.

5. You cannot use paint on the clay.

6. You can use fevicol or adhesive if you want for sticking the clay.

7. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

T-shirt Painting Competition

GC Bounties: 100

  • Rules

1. Only a white T-shirt, fabric colours and paint brushes will be provided by the organisers.

2. Participant Team need to design a T-shirt in accordance with a given theme. Theme will be given by the organiser on spot.

3. Time limit is 3 hours.

4. Maximum size of team is 2.

Face Painting Competition

GC Bounties: 100

  • Rules

1. Team size is of 2 people, one as a painter and the other as the painting.

2. Time limit is 3 hours.

3. Organisers will be providing brushes of all sizes and face paints.

4. People allergic to face paints are not allowed to participate.

5. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

Graffiti Designing Competition

GC Bounties: 100

Graffiti are writing or drawings that have been scribbled, scratched or painted typically illicitly on a wall or other surface.

  • Rules

1. Participants need to make graffiti on a wall based on the ALCHERINGA theme.

2. Organisers will be providing A3 paper, pencils, erasers, sketch pens, painting colours.

3. Time limit is 3 hours.

4. Maximum team size is 4 members.

5. You will be provided a part of wall to make the graffiti on.

Live Sketching Competition

GC Bounties: 100

" The artist is a receptacle for emotions that come from all over the place:from the sky, from the earth, from a scrap of paper, from a spider’s web." -Pablo Picasso

  • Rules

1. To make the sketch only drawing sheet(A4), charcoal pen, chalks and sketching pencils of various grades will be provided by the organisers.

2. No theme.You are allowed to take the items and sketch wherever you want to in the campus and sketch anything live.

3. It is a solo event.

4. You can take required items between 10:00 am to 2:00 pm and you have to submit before 4:00 pm.Submissions after 4:00 pm will not be entertained.

5. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations

Rangoli Making Competition

GC Bounties: 100

  • Rules

1. The participants need to design the rangoli based on the theme given by the organisers on the spot.

2. Maximum team size is of 3 members.

3. Each team will be provided with a space of 2x2 feet for making their rangoli.

4. The participants will be provided with blank sheets, pens/pencils and chalks for designing the rangoli and also with colours for decorating it.

5. Time limit for the competition is 3 hours.

6. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.
