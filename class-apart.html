English Rock Band Competition

As the shredding of the guitars and the beating of the drums picks up steam, heads bang along to the music. A highlight of the festival and an emblem of the talent suffused in the many faces in the crowd, this is one event to hold your breath for. Rock-O-Phonix, the holy grail of rock! The winner takes all...!

  • Rules
  • Pre Finals
  • Finals

1. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/rockophonix.

2. Only English language is allowed.

3. Every team has to submit a link to their best performance.

4. A total of 25 teams will come to Alcheringa for the prefinals

5. Travel reimbursement would be provided as per travel reimbursement policy.

6. Bands need to carry their own instruments. Only a standard drum set without double bass pedal would be provided.

7. Explicit or offensive lyrics/gestures guarantee immediate expulsion from the stage.

8. Booze/drugs/smoke are strictly not allowed in the campus.

9. Decision of judges will be final and binding.

10. Special rewards will be given for best drummer, best vocals, best guitarist, best bassist and best original composition..

1. Each band will have 30 minutes on stage including sound check. They will have to leave the stage after 30 minutes or penalty shall be imposed.

2. Marking scheme is subject to judges.

1. 6 bands would be selected for finals.

2. They would be allocated 45 minutes for performance including sound check.

3. Finalists need to have at least one original composition of minimum duration of 3 minutes.

4. Marking scheme is subject to judges.

Personality Contest

GC Bounties: 200

One of the most anticipated events of the year, brings you one of its own kind personality competition. Are you up for the challenge? Alcheringa awaits to accolade its next Mr Alcheringa and Ms Alcheringa...!!

  • Rules

1. Registrations are done through www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

2. Every participant has to fill the questionnaire whose link will be sent to the email address of the participant.

3. Minimum age limit for the competition is 16 years.

4. Registration form must be completely filled.

5. Candidates will be shortlisted on the basis of their replies to the questionnaire for the Prelims.

6. The selection will be considered final and no requests will be entertained.

7. Requirements of any sort must be informed well beforehand for both Prelims and Finals.

8. Fire/Water/Smoke in any form is not allowed.

9. Props are supposed to be arranged by the participants themselves. (Prop is anything not worn on the body as part of costume eg. bat, stick, hat and/or every piece of costume that is removed during the act).

10. Props Considered hazardous or requiring electricity (eg. fire, fog devices, knives etc.) are not allowed. Throwing Confetti, Chalk Powder, Glitter on the performance area can attract penalty, hence is discouraged and it's the responsibility of participant to clean it.

11. Any music or video file is to be submitted well in advance.

12. Addition or Removal of any round on both the days can be done by the organisers. The decision of the organisers will be considered final and binding in all matters.

13. There will be two rounds namely prelims and finals.

The selection procedure for finals will completely depend on Judges.

1. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.

3. The video content should be raw and original. The use of any special effects is strictly prohibited.

Clash 1:

1. Each rapper will get 1 minute to showcase pre prepared performance. Participants have to bring their own beats for this round.The performance will not be judged.

2. It will help them to know other rappers to diss them in clash 2 and draw support of crowd. Time limit for clash 1: 1 minute.

Clash 2:

1. Rappers will be put in groups depending on number of participants.

2. Participants can diss his/her opponents or can simply showcase the rap on something they have come prepared with.Dissing will get you extra points.

3. Participants have to bring their own beats for this round.

Time limit for clash 2: 2 minute.

Top 10 participants will make it to the finals.

1. Participants will be required to showcase at least two(2) original rap-songs.

2. Participant will get six(6) minutes to showcase a prepared rap performance.

3. Participants can perform in Hindi/English only.

4. Participants have to bring their own beats for this round.

5. The jury decision will be final and binding in all cases.
