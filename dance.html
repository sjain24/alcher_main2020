Street Dance Competition

GC Bounties: 300

Why fight when you can dance? Introducing the most exhilarating, vigorous and effervescent face-off you can imagine! The mean and tough streets are unforgiving. Bring out your game face and show us what you got! Presenting Step Up, Dance face-off!

  • Rules
  • Pre Finals
  • Finals

1. Registrations are done through www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

2. It will be a dance face-off between the two teams.

3. It will be an open –air event.

4. The team must consist of a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 8 members.

5. The minimum age limit for this competition is 12 years.

6. The dance style should be primarily a blend of Hip-Hop, Free styling, break dance, Popping, Locking, B-Boying, Tutting, Waving, Threading and other funky dance styles.

7. The timer for a crew starts at the very moment when the dancer steps into the performing area.

8. The music for the performance will be provided on the spot.

9. The decision taken by the organisers is final and binding for the performers.Any kind of misbehaviour would result in direct disqualification.

10. Follow the general guidelines provided at the end of the module.

1. It is a knock-out based event.The team that wins a round goes to the next level.

2. Teams alternatively take turns to showcase their skills, either through individual or group performance.

3. Each turn lasts for a minute and each team gets 2 such turns.

4. A minimum of 1 and a maximum of 2 props are to be used by the teams during their performance.

1. A coin toss decides which team goes first.

2. The teams will be given 4 turns alternatively, each of 1 minute in the semi-final and the final round.
