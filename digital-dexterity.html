Online Digital Painting Competition

Do you believe you can find a Van Gogh inside you without holding the tip of the brush against the paper? Here is your chance to blend the cursor onto the screen and make the big picture speaks for itself. Set up your mood and composition and explore the translation of going from analog to digital.

Team Size: Solo
Round(s): Direct online finals

JUDGING CRITERIA:
Graphics, Originality, Creativity, technical quality

  • Rules

1. Theme will be declared through our Facebook page.

2. Entries are limited to 2 photos per person.

3. All entries must be in digital format and at the highest resolution possible in a .jpeg, .jpg, or .png format. Files submitted may not be larger than 10 megabytes.

4. All entrants must hold exclusive rights to the photographs they submit and by entering they are claiming such ownership.

5. Participants should be ready with their main work-file and brushes to submit when asked.

Online Doodle Designing Competition

You must have thought how a doodle artist would have started his creation, so this time we provide you a small element of a doodle and all you have to do is to complete that doodle (without editing the given element) from your own creativity

Theme

To be declared through our Facebook page.

  • Rules
  • Judging & Selection Procedures

1. All entries must be in digital format and at the highest resolution possible in a .jpeg, .jpg, or .png format. Files submitted may not be larger than 10 megabytes.

2. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

1. Artistic merit -Drawing skills and use of colour.

2. Creativity and originality -Considering the representation of your theme, as well as the unique and novel approach to the Doodle.

3. Communication of your theme in both the drawing and supporting statement -how well the entrants explain what their doodle represents in the supporting statement and drawing.
