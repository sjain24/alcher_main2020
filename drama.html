Stage Play Competition

GC Bounties: 400

The plethora of expressions, diversity of unimaginable characters, red of the blood all to impress you! Actors from the region will enliven the stage and perform their hearts out to stand victorious in this tough clash of theatre-acting. Welcome to the Stage play competition!

  • Rules
  • Pre Finals
  • Finals

1. Teams have to register at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.

2. The performance must be in Hindi, English.

3. The minimum age limit for this competition is 12 years.

4. Usage of fire/water/smoke in any form is not allowed.

5. The performance can also be a musical drama.

6. Two standing microphones, two collar microphones and four ground microphones will be provided to each team for the performance.

7. Participants must bring their own laptops for sound effects. For any music track or video file, team has to submit it well in advance

8. Live music is also allowed. All required instruments and props must be brought by the team itself. Only two(lights/sound) technicians per team will be allowed.

9. Props are allowed. No props other than chairs and table can be expected from organisers. All the requests for the same must be made a week before prior the performance.

10. Issues like crossing time limits, using abusive language, or vulgarity will lead to negative marking and it will be completely over judge’s discretion.

11. Team must have at least 6 members and a maximum of 20 members.

12. The act should not portray any discrimination or derogratory remark towards any community. Not adhering to this may lead to disqualification

13. The decision of the judges will be binding and final incase of any conflict.

14. Special awards will be given to best actor and best actresses

1. Time limit for prelims is 20 minutes from curtain to curtain (including setting/clearance). A part of the finals will be performed in the prelims.

2. 4 teams will qualify for finals.

1. Time limit (curtain to curtain)for the finals is 50 minutes.

Street Play Competition

GC Bounties: 300

The eloping beats of nagadas and dholkis filling the atmosphere with hysterical enthusiasm are sure to give one dose of live action and stunts amidst a live crowd. The thunderous performances have questions that need to be answered, and answers that will leave you baffled. Halla Bol (Nukkad Natak), a form of theatre which aims at spreading social awareness with style!

  • Rules

1. Teams have to register at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

2. Number of members in a team must not be less than 6.

3. Time limit for the performance is 20 minutes.

4. Usage of fire/water/smoke in any form is not allowed. Musical instruments are allowed.

5. The performance must only be in Hindi, English.

6. The minimum age limit for this competition is 12 years.

7. All the special requests must be made a week before prior the performance.

8. Issues like crossing the time limits, using abusive language, or vulgarity will lead to negative marking and it will be completely over judge’s discretion.

Solo Acting Competition

GC Bounties: 300

It is a one-man/woman show where only one person has to act. Mono acting refers to a single actor playing multiple roles. There is only one actor on stage during mono acting

  • Rules

1. Teams have to register at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.

2. The performance must be in Hindi, English.

3. The minimum age limit for this competition is 12 years.

4. Usage of fire/water/smoke in any form is not allowed.

5. One collar Mic will be provided during performance.

6. Time limit is 5 minutes.

7. Participants must bring their own laptops for sound effects. For any music track or video file, team has to submit it well in advance.

8. Live music is also allowed.All required instruments and props must brought by the participant itself.Only one (lights/sound)technician is allowed if the participant requires it.

9. Props are allowed.All the special requests for the same must be made a week before prior the performance.

10. Issues like crossing time limits, using abusive language, or vulgarity will lead to negative marking and it will be completely over judge's discretion.
