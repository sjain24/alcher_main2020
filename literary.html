Shayari Competition

Just A Minute

GC Bounties: 100

  • Rules

1. The first person to JAM gets to speak.

2. You get points for each second you speak.

3. You get points for each correct interjection you make.

4. You are supposed to speak fluently without any hesitation, stuttering, lisp, grammatical errors or incoherence. At any such interjection any participant can JAM and start speaking
