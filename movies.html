Online Short Movie Making

Tired of the typical cinema?? Do you think that the time has arrived to come out of the clichéd movies and show the world what real movies are about? If you agree and want to show the world your direction skills, then the stage is set for you to bring a change by depicting your ideas with the help of this movie making competition.

  • Rules
  • Judging Criteria

1. The theme as well as the genre of the short movie are not imposed.

2. The short movie should not last for more than 12 minutes (credits included).

3. Suitable for all audience: No obscenity.No Nudity. No Excessive violence.SHORT MOVIE deemed obscene or defamatory will be disqualified.

4. Short movies which were not shot in Hindi or English shall have English subtitles.

5. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.

1. Story telling.

2. Creativity and Originality.

3. Screenplay and Actor’s performance.

4. Technical Execution:Camera/Sound/Light/Editing.

5. Overall impression.

Online Photo Contest

In this digitalised world of today, the best way to make memories is to get clicked.Photography is one of the finest art of present world.Let’s cherish this art via the online photography competition Snapthrillz.From amateurs to professional photographers, every photography lover can participate.

Judging Criteria: Theme implementation, Creativity, Originality

Theme: Stay tuned to our Facebook Page for theme.

  • Rules

1. Photographs of not more than 10 MB in size must be submitted online and the organisers may request for higher resolution images subsequently.

2. Each participant can submit up to three entries.

3. Digital manipulation that distorts the reality of the photos will not be allowed.Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, contrast adjustment, or simple cropping will be allowed.

4. Entries must be the original and unpublished work of the participant and copyright owner.

5. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .
