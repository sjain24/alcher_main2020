Hindi Band Competition

GC Bounties: 400

Come witness an event where the flavours of each part of India find expression through some truly soulful music

  • Rules
  • Pre Finals
  • Finals

1. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.A band will have to submit a link to their songs

2. Vocals of the songs should only be in Hindi.

3. Each band should consist of a minimum of 3 members.

4. Each band should consist of a minimum of 1 vocalist.

5. Bands need to carry their own instruments.Only a standard drum set without double bass pedal would be provided.

6. Explicit or offensive lyrics/gestures guarantee immediate expulsion from the stage.

7. Booze/drugs/smoke are strictly not allowed in campus.

8. Decision of judges will be final and binding.

9. Teams have to submit a video of minimum 3 minutes for online auditions of their previous performances.Entries should be submitted at entries@alcheringa.in.

1. A total of 20 teams will come to Alcheringa for the prefinals.

2. Each band will have 20 minutes on stage including sound check.They will have to leave the stage after 15 minutes or penalty shall be imposed.

3. Marking scheme is subject to judges.

1. The 5 teams will qualify for the finals.

2. Each team will be given a total time of 30 minutes (which also Includes stage setup time).

3. Performance must include one original composition of at least 3 minutes.

Acoustic Band Competition

GC Bounties: 300

Witness the notes being charged up without power, when Alcheringa brings an event without electrical amplification.

  • Rules
  • Prelims
  • Finals

1. Participants can use any instrument of their choice. provided they are acoustic.

2. Vocals are compulsory.

3. The judging will be on the overall performance and not just on the instrumental performance.

4. Mics, one acoustic guitar and a keyboard will be provided.

5. Participants must confirm availability of the instruments in advance with the event organisers.

6. A drum set will not be provided by Alcheringa. Participants are encouraged to use alternate percussion instruments.

7. Participation can be teams of minimum 3 and maximum 5 members.

8. The vocals must be in either Hindi or English.

9. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.

1. Teams will be given 5 minutes of performance time and 2 minutes of setup time.

2. Marking scheme is subject to judges.

1. Top 10 teams will qualify for the finals.

2. Teams will be given 10 minutes of performance time and 3 minutes of setup time.

3. Teams should perform at least 2 songs in the given time.

Solo Singing Competition

GC Bounties: 200

This event is for the lone wolves unafraid to showcase their unique voices.We call out to all the nightingales to soar high this Alcheringa.May the most melodious master prevail!

  • Prelims
  • Finals

1. Registrations are done online at www.alcheringa.in/registrations

2. The participant has to perform any one song of his/her choice.

3. The song must only be in Hindi or English.

4. No instrument is allowed.

5. Participants are not allowed to use any kind of prerecorded music for this round.

6. Maximum performance time for this round is 3 minutes.

7. Top 10 candidates from this round will qualify for the finals.

1 . For this round, the contestants have to choose and prepare the songs beforehand from the list of songs that will be available on our Facebook Page.

2. The selected candidates will perform their selected song along with pre-recorded music, which will be provided to them by the organisers.

3. Maximum performance time for this round will be 10 minutes.
