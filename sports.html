This shorter version of Football is your shot at glory and a chance for you to vindicate your love for the game. Held in one of the football hotspots of the country, this 5-on-5 competition will put your mettle and skills to test unlike any other. Come, play your best shot!

  • Rules

1. A total of 64 teams for Boys will be registered.

2. Minimum age limit is 15 years.

3. Online registrations are advised. Offline registration is subject to availability and will be strictly based on "First Come First Serve" basis. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

4. Participants may register as teams of a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 7 members.

5. Only 5 players per team (including the goalkeeper) will be allowed to play at a time.

6. Matches will constitute of two halves of 10 minutes, each with a 4-minute break in between. However, a match will be over then and there if a team manages to get a 3-goal lead over the opponent.

7. In case of a tie, teams will play sudden death "golden goal" in extra time to determine the winner over 2 halves of 4 minutes each. A draw at the end of Extra Time will be decided by a Penalty Shootout.

8. The Off-side rule will ‘NOT’ be followed.

9. Players will not be allowed to play barefoot. Use of normal sport shoes or football studs is allowed. We also encourage you to get your own shin guards, which are of prime importance for your safety.

10. Rolling Substitutions will be allowed.

11. No player can register with more than one team during the entire tournament. Also teams once knocked out will not be registered again.

12. The dimensions of the field will be 91.4m x 55m.

13. Possession of any sharp or metal object that can cause any injury during the play will directly lead to the disqualification of the team.

14. The decision of the Match Referees will be binding on all issues, and will not be subject to any post match reviews.

15. All other rules will essentially be followed according to FIFA standards.

16. The tournament director reserves the right to alter the rules and/or schedule in case of unforeseen circumstances.

This shorter version of Basketball is your shot at glory and a chance for you to vindicate your love for the game. Come, play your best shot!

  • Rules
  • Common Rules

1. The game is played on a half-court, with one basket. A size 7 standard (749–780 mm/29.5–30.7 in) court will be used for the matches.

2. The game is a single period of 10 minutes with sudden death at 21 points. The winner is the first team to score 21 or the team with the highest score at the end of the 10 minutes.

1. Online registrations are advised. Offline registration is subject to availability and will be strictly based on "First Come First Serve" basis. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

2. Each team consists of three players and one substitute.

3. The minimum age limit for this competition is 15 years.

4. A coin toss will be held immediately before the game. The winning team can choose to take possession of the ball at the start of the game, or take the first possession of a potential overtime period.

5. There are no jump balls at any time in the game; neither is there an alternating possession rule. In any held ball situation, the defensive team is granted possession. Every successful shot inside the arc is awarded one point, while every successful shot behind the arc is awarded two points.

6. A tie in regulation leads to an untimed overtime period, which is won by the first team to score points in overtime. If a foul is committed that results in the non-fouling team retaining possession i.e., a technical or "unsportsmanlike" foul - the non-fouling team will receive the exchange.

7. A 12-second shot clock is used.If the defence gains possession of the ball within the arc, by a steal, a block or a rebound, the team must move the ball behind the arc before being allowed to take a shot.

8. After a made basket or free throw, play restarts with a player from the non-scoring team taking the ball directly under the basket and dribbling or passing it to a point behind the arc.The defence is not allowed to play for the ball inside the block/charge semi-circle under the basket.

9. Each team is allowed one timeout (The officials may still stop the game in case of player injury or other dangerous situations).Substitution is allowed only during a dead-ball situation.Individual personal foul counts are not kept.

10. In other words, players cannot be disqualified on the basis of personal fouls.However, a player who commits two unsportsmanlike fouls is disqualified.Fouls during the act of shooting inside the arc are awarded 1 free throw, whilst fouls during the act of shooting behind the arc are awarded 2 free throws.Technical fouls (including unsportsmanlike fouls)result in two free throws plus possession of the ball.

This shorter version of Volleyball is your shot at glory and a chance for you to vindicate your love for the game. Come, play your best shot!

  • Rules

1. Online registrations are advised. Offline registration is subject to availability and will be strictly based on "First Come First Serve" basis. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations .

2. Standard Volleyball rules will be followed otherwise modified.

3. Every team will consist of 3 members.

4. It’s a mix event, i.e., girls and boys can be in the same team.

5. Every drop and foul will be given 1 point.

6. The game is a single period of 10 minutes with sudden death at 11 points. The winner is the first team to score 11 points or the team with the highest score at the end of the 10 minutes.

7. In case of a tie at the end of 10 minutes, extra 3 minutes will be given. If there is a tie even after 3 minutes, a coin toss will decide the winner.
