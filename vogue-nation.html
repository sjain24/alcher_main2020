Paper Dress Designing Competition

GC Bounties: 200

Design the best outfit for your friend using trash. Use your origami and fashion skills to make the perfect dress!

  • Rules

1. This is a team event. Each team will have 3 members, one person, for whom the outfit will be designed and the others designer for the outfit.

2. No material (fresh/waste) is allowed to be used which is brought from outside the campus.

3. Teams are needed to make a dress from newspaper and other logistics provided on the spot. Newspapers, A4 sheets, ribbons, stapler, pins, cello tape, sketch pens will be given to each team.

4. Theme will be provided on the spot.

5. Time limit is 3 hours.

6. Registrations are done at www.alcheringa.in/registrations
